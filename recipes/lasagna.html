Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian food made from sheets of pasta. It's name comes from the Latin lasanum "a cooking pot".

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato puree
  • Tinned tomato
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the meat and onions
  2. Stir in tomato
  3. Layer with pasta sheets and cheese
  4. Cook until it looks fit
