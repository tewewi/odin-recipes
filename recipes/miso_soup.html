Miso Soup Recipe

Description

Miso Soup is a Japanese soup made from dashi stock and tofu. It's name comes from the miso paste it is made from.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Miso paste
  • Green onions
  1. Boil dashi granules in water
  2. Whisk in miso paste
  3. Add chopped tofu
  4. Add green onions to make it look fit
